I am honoured to have received the **Best Video Award** at the **21st International Conference on Advanced Robotics (ICAR 2023)**, held in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

🔗 [website](https://www.mech.kuleuven.be/en/pma/research/ras/rasnews/ayoob-davoodi-wins-best-video-award-at-icar-2023-in-abu-dhabi-uae)

## About the Award

ICAR is one of the most established robotics conferences, covering areas such as:

- Robotic vision  
- Cognitive robotics  
- Medical robotics  
- Autonomous systems  

The Best Video Award recognises outstanding research demonstrations that clearly communicate both **technical innovation and real-world impact**.

## Awarded Work

**"A Comparative Study for Control of Semi-Automatic Robotic-assisted Ultrasound System in Spine Surgery"**

Authors:  
Ayoob Davoodi, Ruixuan Li, Yuyu Cai, Kenan Niu, Gianni Borghesan, Emmanuel Vander Poorten

## Contribution

This work focused on:

- Control strategies for robotic ultrasound systems  
- Real-time integration of sensing and control  
- Applications in minimally invasive spine surgery  

## Significance

This award highlights the importance of combining **technical excellence with effective scientific communication**, especially in robotics research.
