I am grateful to have received a **Research Mobility Grant** from the **Professor R. Snoeys Foundation**. This grant supports outstanding researchers by enabling international collaboration and experimental research.

🔗 [website](https://www.mech.kuleuven.be/en/pma/research/ras/rasnews/professor-r-snoeys-foundation-awarded-ayoob-davoodi-and-kaat-van-assche-a-travel-grant-each)

## Research Collaboration

As part of the **FAROS project**, we collaborated with:

- University of Zurich (UZH)  
- Balgrist University Hospital  

The grant supported a research stay in Zurich, Switzerland, allowing us to:

- Conduct experiments in a clinical environment  
- Work closely with surgeons and clinicians  
- Advance robotic technologies for spine surgery  

## Impact

This collaboration strengthened the connection between:

- Robotics research  
- Clinical practice  

and contributed to the development of **real-world, clinically relevant robotic systems**.

We sincerely thank the Professor R. Snoeys Foundation for their support and trust.
