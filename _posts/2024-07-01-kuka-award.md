I am proud to share that our team **ULTRATOPIA** has won the **KUKA Innovation Award 2025**, a prestigious international competition in medical robotics.

🔗 [website](https://www.kuka.com/innovationaward2025)



## About the Award

The KUKA Innovation Award focuses on innovative robotic solutions in healthcare, including applications in diagnostics, rehabilitation, and surgery. The 2025 challenge highlighted **human–robot interaction and future medical applications**.



## Our Contribution

Our team (KU Leuven and Balgrist University Hospital, University of Zurich) developed an advanced robotic system for:

- Ultrasound-guided spine surgery  
- Collaborative multi-robot systems  
- Radiation-free pedicle screw placement  

The system integrates:

- A robotic arm equipped with ultrasound imaging  
- A robotic drilling system guided in real time  



## Impact

This approach improves:

- Surgical accuracy  
- Robustness to physiological motion  
- Safety by reducing radiation exposure  

Winning this award demonstrates the strong potential of our work to **advance robot-assisted spine surgery toward clinical practice**.
