## Overview

AIRCARE is a Horizon Europe project aimed at transforming diagnosis and treatment of Upper Aerodigestive Tract (UADT) cancers through AI and robotics.

## Contributions

- Developed teleoperated and haptic control for surgical robotics  
- Integrated AI-driven perception into clinical workflows  
- Contributed to robotic system design and validation  

## Impact

- ~€8M EU-funded project  
- Translational medical robotics application
