## Overview

FAROS aims to improve surgical accuracy by embedding physical intelligence into robotic systems.

## Contributions

- Developed ultrasound-guided robotic navigation for spine surgery  
- Designed dual-arm robotic system for drilling guidance  
- Conducted ex-vivo, in-vivo, and cadaver experiments  

## Impact

- EU Horizon 2020 funded project  
- Advanced surgical robotics toward clinical use
