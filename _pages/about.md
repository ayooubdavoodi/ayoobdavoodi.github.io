I am robotics and mechatronics researcher in Robot-Assisted Surgery at KU Leuven with more than 6 years of experience in
robot-assisted systems, control, and real-time sensing integration.

My work focuses on translating advanced robotic systems into real-world clinical
applications, especially in **robot-assisted surgery**, **AI-based robot control**,
and **ultrasound-guided robotics**.

## Research & Experience

- EU-funded research projects (Horizon 2020 & Horizon Europe)  
- Robotic system design, integration, and experimental validation  
- Ultrasound-guided robotic navigation for spine surgery  
- Real-time control, sensing, and AI-based motion compensation  
- Supervision of MSc students and multidisciplinary teamwork

- ⚡ **Adaptability**: Comfortable working across robotics, AI, and medical systems.
- 🤝 **Collaboration**: Experienced in multidisciplinary teamwork and supervision.
